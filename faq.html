Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I make a reservation?

To make a reservation, simply visit our website and select your desired car, rental dates, and other details in the booking form. You can also contact our customer service team for assistance with your reservation.

2. What are the age requirements for renting a car?

To rent a car with us, you must be at least 25 years old and hold a valid driver's license. Additional age requirements may apply for specific car models. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for more information.

3. Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation. The cancellation policy may vary based on the rental duration and car category. Please review our Terms and Conditions for detailed information about the cancellation policy.

4. Do you offer airport pickup and drop-off services?

Yes, we offer airport pickup and drop-off services for your convenience. Please provide your flight details while making the reservation, so we can arrange the service accordingly.

5. Are there any additional fees?

Additional fees may apply for services such as GPS navigation, child seats, and additional drivers. These fees will be clearly mentioned during the reservation process.

6. What if I need to extend my rental period?

If you need to extend your rental period, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. Extensions are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

7. Can I rent a specific car model?

Yes, you can specify the car model you wish to rent during the reservation process. However, availability is subject to the car's availability on the requested dates.

8. What documents do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you'll need a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and a valid credit card for the security deposit. International renters will also need a valid passport and international driver's license.

9. How do I pay for the rental?

You can pay for the rental online using a credit or debit card. We accept major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express.

10. What if the car breaks down during my rental?

In the unlikely event of a car breakdown, contact our 24/7 customer service team immediately. We'll provide assistance and arrange for a replacement vehicle if necessary.
